About Era Bistro

Era Bistro is a vintage-themed global kitchen located in Film Nagar, Hyderabad. The restaurant offers Italian and international cuisine in a cozy, trendy atmosphere. It is popular for lunch, dinner, solo dining, and group gatherings. Highlights include rooftop seating, fireplace ambiance, great desserts, and aesthetic interiors. The restaurant supports dine-in, takeaway, delivery, and reservations. Amenities include free Wi-Fi, gender-neutral restrooms, valet parking, and ample parking space. Family-friendly, LGBTQ+ friendly, and ideal for both casual and special occasions.
